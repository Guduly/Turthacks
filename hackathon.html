Blinds Light Sensor

The BLS (Blinds Light sensor) detects light inside indoor environments and utilizes the photoelectric effect to send electrical output to the smart Blinds; which through the power of IOT could be able to close the blinds by itself. The BLS could be automated to start at a certain time, this would allow for less human interaction and increase consistancy in light levels. This consistenty in light levels, could decrease light pollution, which in turn decreases CO2 emmisions and disruption of ecosystems.
